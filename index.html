Udupi is a coastal district in the Indian state of Karnataka, known for its beautiful beaches, temples, and educational institutions. Udupi is a simplified and peaceful version of Goa, with stunning beaches like Malpe and Kaup. Udupi is also famous for its vegetarian food, served in the South Indian style, which can be found in Udupi restaurants all over India. Udupi is a place of delight which brings happiness with its natural beauty.
